Services

Airport pick-up and transfer

We arrange airport pick-ups and transfers to and from your hotel. The moment you land at Entebbe airport, you are met by our meet and greet service staff who will accompany you to your hotel and make sure that you have settled in.

Wildpath Expeditions will arrange hotel bookings and reservations anywhere in the country. Accommodation in Uganda includes boutique hotels, upmarket safari lodges, mid-range, and budget hotels.
